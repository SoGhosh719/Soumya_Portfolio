From Chemistry to AI: My Journey of Transformation

The Beginning: A Passion for Science and Numbers

My academic roots lie in chemistry, where I honed my analytical mindset and problem-solving abilities. Studying qualitative and quantitative analysis instilled in me a love for patterns, precision, and data-driven decision-making. However, I soon realized that my true passion extended beyond just understanding chemical reactions—I was drawn to the world of analytics, business strategy, and computational intelligence.

A Shift in Perspective: Diving Into Business Analytics

Seeking to merge my analytical skills with real-world applications, I pursued a Post-Graduate Diploma in Business Analytics at the Indian Institute of Social Welfare and Business Management. Here, I was introduced to database management, statistics, and machine learning—fields that would later define my career. My capstone project, an age detection model for Indian actors, was my first deep dive into AI. Using the Indian Movie Face Database (IMFDB) and training a machine learning model, I achieved 91% accuracy in predicting age categories. It was a thrilling realization that data, when structured and analyzed effectively, could drive powerful insights and solutions.

Bridging Data and Business: Real-World Impact

Upon entering the professional world, I took on roles that allowed me to combine data-driven decision-making with business strategy. At HDFC ERGO General Insurance, I managed a diverse client portfolio, where I successfully increased policy sales by 20% within the first year. My ability to interpret consumer data and tailor personalized financial solutions enabled me to exceed monthly sales targets by 90%, a feat that earned recognition from senior management. Later, as a Business Operations Manager and Analyst at Bharti AXA Life Insurance, I leveraged Generative AI to optimize workflows, increasing productivity by 31% and driving a $52K revenue boost. The power of AI-driven automation and data analytics transformed not only our operational efficiency but also our approach to customer retention and engagement.

Exploring AI and Machine Learning: Research and Innovation

My research stint at Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Kanpur through the iSMRITI Program further fueled my interest in AI applications. Here, I developed and deployed a Flask-based hybrid recommender system, integrating collaborative and content-based filtering. By using TF-IDF vectorization, cosine similarity, and Pearson correlation, we improved recommendation accuracy to 86.7%. This experience reinforced my belief that machine learning, when applied correctly, could revolutionize industries beyond just tech—whether in finance, healthcare, or entertainment.

Business Analytics at Clark University: Expanding My Horizons

To refine my expertise and gain a global perspective, I pursued a Master of Science in Business Analytics at Clark University. The rigorous coursework in machine learning, predictive analytics, business intelligence, and statistical modeling has helped me bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical implementation. Throughout my time at Clark, I have tackled various analytical projects that have deepened my understanding of data science applications: Hospital Readmission Analysis – Developed predictive models using XGBoost (ROC-AUC: 0.69) to optimize patient readmission predictions. Hanover Theatre Marketing Strategies – Designed personalized marketing strategies to enhance student engagement and inclusivity. Future Price Prediction of Commodities – Created a hybrid ARIMA-Random Forest model (95% accuracy) to predict commodity price fluctuations. Football Market Value Analysis – Conducted in-depth statistical modeling to evaluate player performance and market trends in the English Premier League.

The Road Ahead: A Vision for AI-Driven Business Solutions

My journey has been a testament to the power of adaptability. From chemistry to finance, from insurance to AI, each experience has contributed to my ever-evolving skill set. My goal is to leverage AI, data analytics, and business strategy to drive intelligent decision-making, optimize business operations, and improve user experiences. As I look toward the future, I am eager to explore opportunities where I can apply my expertise in AI, GenAI, and data-driven business strategies to create tangible impact. Whether it's enhancing supply chain efficiency, transforming financial services, or advancing AI applications in healthcare, I believe that the fusion of data, business, and technology holds boundless potential.

A Message to Aspiring Professionals

If there’s one lesson my journey has taught me, it’s that learning never stops. The ability to pivot, acquire new skills, and stay curious is what defines growth. If you’re transitioning careers or exploring a new domain, don’t hesitate to take that first step. Every challenge, every experiment, and every project is a stepping stone to something greater. And so, my journey continues. The destination is undefined, but the possibilities are limitless.
